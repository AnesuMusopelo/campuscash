FAQ

Frequently asked questions for the Campus Cash demo product.

How much can a student borrow?

In this demo, applications range from BWP 300 to BWP 2,500.

How long is the loan term?

The demo uses a 30-day repayment window from the approval date.

What is the pricing model?

The demo shows a fixed 25% fee over 30 days.

Can this process real repayments?

No. This version is a presentation-quality front-end demo without payment rails.

Does it save data?

Yes, the browser stores demo applications using localStorage on your device.

Can it be expanded?

Yes. A future version could add real authentication, backend APIs, payments, KYC, and collections workflows.
