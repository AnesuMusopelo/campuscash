FAQ

Helpful answers for demo presentations and concept walkthroughs.

What does Campus Cash offer?

A 30-day student micro-loan concept for Botswana with a flat 25% fee.

Is this a real lender?

No. This is a front-end prototype for showcasing product design and user journeys.

How are decisions made?

The demo uses simple affordability and completeness checks, with manual admin review available.

Can it work on mobile?

Yes. This redesign is deliberately mobile-first and also scales up cleanly on desktop browsers.
